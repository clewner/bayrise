ABOUT US

BayRise is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to creating positive change in the Bay Area. Through providing enrichment programs to underprivileged youths, we hope to inspire the next generation of innovators, creators, and leaders to pursue their passions and reach their full potentials.

OUR START

What began with trash pickups and teaching relatives about computers at the end of 2021, has quickly expanded into a full-fledged organization, spanning different cities and reaching many kids and teens.

MEET THE TEAM

Our team consists of a group high schoolers from different schools in the Bay Area with one trait in common: the desire to improve our home.
