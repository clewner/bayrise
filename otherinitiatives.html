Bimonthly Warming Campaign

Every two months, we purchase blankets and warm clothing in bulk at discounted rates from NorthEast Fleece and CheapWinterDeals using funds raised from our community. We distribute these items to unhoused individuals in Berkeley, Oakland, and San Francisco through various channels, such as soup kitchens, community centers, and homeless shelters.

Our goal is to offer relief and warmth during the cold winter months while also reminding the homeless community that they are not alone. Through our efforts, we hope to make a positive impact on the lives of those we serve by fostering a sense of community and support.

Trash Pickups

We organize trash pickups around Berkeley and Oakland where we see fit, trying to get the youth of the community involved as well as adults. We believe that instilling environmental values in young people is crucial for creating a sustainable future. Through our efforts, we hope to inspire others to take action and make a positive impact in their own communities.
